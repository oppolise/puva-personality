# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Thai voters, students, and politically curious social-media users who want a quick, playful quiz about Bangkok governor archetypes. They are likely on mobile, sharing screenshots or links with friends, and expecting a lightweight experience rather than a serious voter guide.

## Product Purpose

4 ผู้ว่า 4 You is a static parody personality quiz that turns everyday life choices into four fictionalized governor-style personas. Success means users can finish the quiz quickly, recognize their own habits in the answer choices, laugh without feeling misled, and share their result without the site appearing to be official campaign material or advice.

## Brand Personality

Playful, civic, sharp. The interface should feel like a clean social quiz with a Bangkok-specific wink: clear enough for real policy topics, unserious enough to be visibly satire.

## Anti-references

Do not look like an official election tool, a real candidate endorsement site, or a clone of 16Personalities. Avoid overly long policy paragraphs, choices that expose the scoring too obviously, UI that feels like a government form, and jokes that overpower the policy scenario.

## Design Principles

- Keep the quiz fast: one clear everyday situation, four readable choices, no explanation tax.
- Make the parody explicit but not sloppy: the user should understand the joke and the personality cue at the same time.
- Mobile first: large tap targets, short lines, and hierarchy that survives small screens.
- Result pages should be clean and shareable, with the character as the hero.
- Privacy is part of the product promise: no backend, no analytics, no local storage, no answer export.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for readable Thai typography on mobile, high contrast body text, touch targets at least 44px high, and no essential information conveyed by color alone. Motion should remain minimal and non-blocking.
